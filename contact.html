About Car-ibu

Car-ibu is a user-friendly car search platform designed to help users discover detailed information about various vehicle models. Our mission is to simplify car research and make car shopping engaging, fun, and informative.

What We Offer

  • 🔍 Search for vehicles by make
  • 🖼️ Get images and key details for every model
  • ❤️ Save your favorites to a Wishlist
  • 🕘 Keep track of your recent searches

Meet the Team

We are a passionate group of developers and car enthusiasts. Car-ibu was created to merge our love for cars with technology, offering users a better way to explore the automotive world.
